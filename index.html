OUR INGREDIENTS

Here at Old Spice, we constantly strive to provide the best personal care products, while also respecting and protecting our environment. Ingredients like aluminum, volatile organic compounds (VOCs), propane and butane are harmful for both you and the environment. We are actively avoiding such ingredients and fight to educate you on their harmful effects.

YOU SHOULDN’T USE
DEODORANTS

This is a personal prefference. Deodorants eliminate armpit odor, but not perspiration. Antiperspirants temporarily block sweat pores and reduce perspiration. The choice is all yours!

AEROSOLS ARE BAD FOR
THE ENVIRONMENT

True. Aerosol deodorants contain gas propellants that contribute to air pollution and climate change. We have removed these propellants so you can safely enjoy our products.

OUR MISSION

In our race to uncovering what makes you smell like a man the most, we discovered manlihood doesn’t have a specific smell. We realised what you smell like doesn’t define who you are. The choices you make, however, do. We chose to be more sustainable and help create a safer world for the years to come.

We focus on finding the most enviromentally and user-friendly alternatives for our product line, and educating the public on safe practices when it comes to their personal care and product choices.
